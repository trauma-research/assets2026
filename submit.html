Submit to the workshop

Co-creating a Trauma-Informed Accessibility Research Practice. Submit a position paper, a multimodal short story, or a visual or creative contribution.

Submission deadline: 7 August 2026, Anywhere on Earth (AoE). Notification: 28 August 2026. Workshop day and format To be confirmed.

Submissions are collected through a Google Form. Read what to prepare below, then open the submission form. All submissions must comply with the SIGACCESS accessibility guidelines; we will work with you to make your submission fully accessible before it is shared with participants. At least one author of each accepted submission must attend the workshop in person and be registered for the ASSETS 2026 conference by the early registration deadline.

What you can submit

Choose the format that best fits your contribution:

  • Position paper — 2–4 pages, ACM format. Your research, practice, or perspective on trauma-informed research in accessibility or HCI.
  • Multimodal short story — up to 4 pages. A lived experience of trauma as it relates to research participation or conduct, through narrative, image, or another format, with a brief reflexive statement.
  • Visual or creative submission — a poster, diagram, or illustrated narrative, plus a one-page description explaining the work and its relevance to the workshop themes.

What to prepare

Before you open the form, have the following ready. The form will ask for:

  • Your submission title and type.
  • Your submission file (PDF preferred).
  • An optional short description (up to 150 words) and any related workshop themes.
  • The contact author's name, email, and affiliation. List all authors in the file itself.
  • Any access requirements — for the submission process now, or for the workshop. Tell us early; we will ask again if your submission is accepted.
  • Confirmation that at least one author will attend in person and register for ASSETS 2026, and that you will work with us on accessibility.
